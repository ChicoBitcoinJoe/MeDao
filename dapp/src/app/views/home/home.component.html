How does it work?

Having a MeDao makes you an employee of the world. Investors pool money together from which you (the employee) withdraw a salary. You then use the time to produce value that is eventually returned to the investors.

Is it safe?

A MeDao has built in properties to protect the funds of each investor. Downside potential is something that can be calculated down to the penny. For example, an investor who invests $100 into a 33 year old employee can know that in exactly one year, their investment will still be worth $97 even if no value is created by the employee.
